Alpina Rules

Goal of the game

Score the most victory points by strategically placing your Hikers on the cards.

Game setup

  • Each player receives 6 cards.
  • Number of Hikers: 2 players (8), 3 players (5), 4 players (4).
  • Grid: 5x5 with a 'Chamois' card in the center.

Turn overview

Play a card (MANDATORY):

  • Place horizontally or vertically adjacent to an existing card.
  • Must not form a 6th row or column.

Place a Hiker (OPTIONAL):

  • Card must be empty.
  • Adjacent to the card you just placed.
